Pakistani Festivals

Pakistan is home to a variety of vibrant festivals celebrated throughout the year. Some of the most significant ones include:

  • Eid-ul-Fitr - Celebrated at the end of Ramadan with special meals and gifts.
  • Eid-ul-Azha - Observed by sacrificing animals and sharing the meat with those in need.
  • Eid Milad-un-Nabi - Marks the birth of Prophet Muhammad with prayers and community events.
Eid-ul-Fitr

Celebrate the end of Ramadan with family and friends. Enjoy special meals and exchange gifts.

Eid-ul-Azha

Observe the sacrifice of Ibrahim with prayers and festivities. Share the meat with those in need.

Eid Milad-un-Nabi

Celebrate the birth of Prophet Muhammad with gatherings, prayers, and community events.
